Our Ethos


At Park Boxing London, we are passionate about boxing and fitness. We believe Boxing is a sport for everyone! Regardless of your age, gender or occupation, boxing could be the sport for you. We approach each client differently and tailor your sessions according to your own personal goals, experience and fitness level.

Whether you want to box competitively, improve your general fitness or just learn a new skill - we can help you achieve that.


Our Trainers

Aidan

South African born, Aidan is a lover of old school boxing legends like Joe Frazier, George Foreman and Mike Tyson. He tries to emulate these legends through his tough, gritty style of boxing - focusing on physical conditioning and explosiveness. Aidan is a boxer-puncher, utilizing sharp counter punches and devastating hooks to attack his opponents.

- Has been a trainer for over 3 years.

James

Also born in South Africa, James learned boxing at a young age, wanting to follow in the footsteps of old school South African boxing legends like Corrie Sanders. James admires fighters like James Tony, Floyd mayweather and Lennox Lewis. James' style focuses on technique and footwork to set up punches. He enjoys using slick techniques to find openings and outclass his opponents.

- Has been a trainer for 3 years.
